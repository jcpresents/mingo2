MINGO Q&A

Welcome to MINGO! The only true movie bingo challenge for the modern era! Join us in an (extremely) humble celebration of the movies we love.

On a muggy day in May MINGO started as a community effort to revisit select internalized movies that just don't get made anymore. MINGO was just a piece of paper with titles for years until 2018 when The List was first pictoralized. Once a year we gather to celebrate the past and the cinema classics that gets stuck in our brain and just won't leave. Enjoy the MINGO season, a summer tradition with no end in sight!

MINGO is a game of champions, where only the strong survive. But while the challenge is grueling, the rules are simple, and there are many ways to play the game. Choose the MINGO that suits you and then prepare yourself as best you can!

Universal Rules:

Achieve a Bingo by watching a full set of 4-5 movies.

Additionally, the challenge must be completed within a month.

Classic:

Choose any contiguous line of movies (including diagonal), then watch them in any order.

Supreme:

Choose a line of four movies intersecting the free space, but substitute a MINGO movie of your own in its place!

Free-Form:

Choose any five movies, then watch them in any order.

The Forbidden MINGO:

Choose TWO lines (row, column, or diagonal) of movies and watch them all.

What makes a MINGO movie? It's simple: these are movies we haven't seen in a while, and would like to experience anew. This list has been painstakingly selected to provide a diversity of content to all participants--including myself. And yet, all these films have one thing in commmon: Someone remembers them fondly. And with any luck, you'll remember them fondly as well! And so it begins...
